headphone-recommender

Takes in a mp3 song and recommends 100+ headphones based on price, form factor, and sound signature (bassy, neutral, mid, v-shaped). Uses the Fourier Transform and Welch's method on the mp3 to generate a spectral density estimation of the song, then classifies it and returns a list of headphones for the user.
